Event Fencing FAQ

How far in advance should I book event fencing in Green Bay?
We recommend booking temporary event fencing at least 1–2 weeks before your event, especially for summer events when demand is high across the Fox Valley. That said, we can often accommodate last-minute requests — call 920-858-9957 and we'll let you know what we can do.
How long does event fence setup take?
Setup time depends on total linear footage. A 300-foot event perimeter typically takes 1.5–2 hours. We schedule arrival so fencing is fully installed before your event opens. We can also set up the day before if your venue allows early access.
Can you remove event fencing the same day the event ends?
Yes. We coordinate removal around your event schedule and can have a crew out the same evening or first thing the next morning. Fast removal is important for venues with tight turnaround schedules.
Do you serve events outside Green Bay?
We serve events across Northeast Wisconsin including Appleton, Oshkosh, Manitowoc, Sheboygan, and Door County. If you're organizing an outdoor event anywhere in the Fox Valley or along Lake Michigan, call us for availability. See our full service area.

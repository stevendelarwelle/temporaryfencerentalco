How long does installation take on a typical construction site?
Installation time depends on perimeter length. A 200-foot construction site perimeter typically takes 1–2 hours to set up. Larger sites with multiple gate openings may take longer. We schedule installation around your crew's arrival so the site is secured from day one.
Can I move panels around as my construction site changes?
Yes. That's one of the advantages of our panel-based temporary construction fencing. The weighted base system means panels can be repositioned without ground penetration. Call us if your layout changes significantly and we'll come out to reconfigure.
